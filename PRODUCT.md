# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

PM0 is for founders, product-minded builders, PMs, operators, and product engineers using AI coding agents. They work in young teams where product context lives across calls, tickets, docs, analytics, repo history, and founder memory. They need a way to turn that scattered context into product decisions and agent-ready artifacts without adopting a heavy PM process.

## Product Purpose

PM0 is an installable skill that creates repo-native product memory in `.pm0/`, helps agents analyze product surfaces before changing code, and turns loose product input into proposals, prototypes, and engineering-ready PRDs. Success means visitors quickly understand that PM0 is open source, installable, practical, and built for product judgment before code work.

## Brand Personality

Pragmatic, founder-grade, and repo-native. The brand should feel like a product operator's field notebook merged with a command-line tool: direct, useful, opinionated, and grounded in real product mess.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI SaaS gloss, waitlist or request-access framing, inflated platform claims, decorative dashboards, and landing pages that hide the actual install path behind marketing abstraction. Avoid making PM0 feel like a full product-management suite, a ticket tracker, or a replacement for engineering review.

## Design Principles

1. Lead with the product loop, not vague AI promise.
2. Make the install path and command language feel central, tactile, and trustworthy.
3. Show scattered inputs becoming structured product work.
4. Keep the copy concise, founder-readable, and skeptical of process theater.
5. Preserve open-source credibility by making source access and inspection obvious.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Use semantic sections, strong keyboard focus states, reduced-motion fallbacks for the physics hero, readable contrast, and responsive layouts that preserve the command, source links, and core explanation on small screens.
